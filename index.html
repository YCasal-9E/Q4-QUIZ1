BMI

Body Mass Index (BMI) is a simple index of weight-for-height commonly used to classify underweight, normal weight, overweight, and obesity.

Instructions
BMI Instructions:
  • Weight should be entered in kilograms (kg).
  • Height should be entered in meters (m).
  • BMI is calculated by dividing your weight by your height squared (BMI = weight / height²).
  • The calculator will then display your BMI value along with the corresponding category.
BMI Categories:
Category BMI Range
Underweight BMI < 18.5
Normal weight 18.5 ≤ BMI ≤ 24.9
Overweight 25 ≤ BMI ≤ 29.9
Obese BMI ≥ 30
